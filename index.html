WELCOME TO MY PORTFOLIO

Greetings! I am Sidhant Das, a passionate explorer in the realm of Robotics and Autonomous Systems. With a profound background in Masters in Robotics and Autonomous Systems, I am delighted to share my journey and aspirations with you.

My fascination for the cutting-edge field of robotics has led me to focus on areas that bridge the gap between humans and machines. I find immense joy and fulfillment in exploring the intricacies of Human-Robotic Interaction and the potential of Exoskeletons to augment human capabilities.

Having recently completed my Masters, I am now actively seeking to apply my knowledge and skills in a meaningful role. Currently, I am also contributing as a volunteer at the Neuromuscular Controls and Human Robotics Laboratory at ASU, honing my expertise in this ever-evolving field.

I am excited to explore new challenges and contribute my creativity and technical acumen to projects that have a lasting impact. If you are looking for a dedicated and forward-thinking robotics enthusiast, I would be thrilled to connect and discuss how I can contribute to your organization's mission.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. Let's explore the possibilities together!
